1. Introduction & Grounds for Data Collection

This Privacy Policy outlines Sherman SEO ("we", "our" or "the Company") practices with respect to information collected from users who access our website at shermanseo.com ("Site"), or otherwise share personal information with us (collectively: "Users").

Processing of your personal information is necessary for:

  • Performance of our contractual obligations towards you

  • Protecting our legitimate interests
  • Compliance with legal and financial regulatory obligations

When you use the Site, you consent to the collection, storage, use, disclosure and other uses of your Personal Information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect two types of data and information from Users:

📊 Non-Personal Information

Un-identified and non-identifiable information that may include:

  • Aggregated usage information
  • Technical information (browser type, OS, language preference)
  • Site activity (pages viewed, clicks, actions)
  • Access time and browsing patterns

👤 Personal Information

Individually identifiable information including:

  • Device Information: Geolocation data, IP address, unique identifiers (MAC address, UUID)
  • Registration Information: Full name, email, telephone, business name
  • Appointment Details: Scheduling information and preferences

We receive your Personal Information from various sources:

7. Cookies Policy

We and our trusted partners use cookies and other technologies when you visit our Site or access our services.

A "cookie" is a small piece of information that a website assigns to your device while viewing a website. Cookies help with navigation, feature activation, preference remembering, and making interactions quicker and easier.

🍪 Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Session Cookies: Stored temporarily during browsing, deleted when browser closes
  • Persistent Cookies: Saved for a fixed period, used for repeat visits and preferences
  • Third Party Cookies: Set by analytics companies and other online services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about Site usage, including access frequency and page visits. This information helps us improve our Site and services. Google Analytics collects IP addresses rather than personally identifying information, and we don't combine this data with personally identifiable information.
